How to Use

1

Format Your Questions

Use the following markdown format for your quiz questions:

### **Chapter 1: Topic Name**

1. What is 2+3?  (Multiple Choice)
   a) 6
   b) 1
   *c) 5 (asterisk marks correct)
   d) 10

2. Which are dinosaurs?  (Multiple Answers)
   [ ] Woolly mammoth
   [*] Tyrannosaurus rex
   [*] Triceratops

3. Who lives at North Pole?  (Short Answer)
   * Santa
   * Santa Claus
   * Father Christmas

4. Water is liquid.  (True/False)
   *a) True
   b) False

5. Write an essay.  (Essay)
####

6. Upload a file.  (File Upload)
^^^^

Note: Mark correct answers with asterisk (*) for MC/TF, [*] for multiple answers.

2

Generate QTI Package

Paste your formatted questions into the input area and click "Generate QTI Package" to download a ZIP file.
